About EcoVerse

Developed by Team Dynamics

EcoVerse is an all-in-one smart sustainability platform developed by Team Dynamics to promote environmental responsibility across college campuses. It combines smart sensors, QR code logging, and AI-powered waste classification into a single intuitive app that encourages eco-friendly habits through gamification.

🌱 Key Features:

  • ♻️ PlastiLoop – Detects plastic waste drops and rewards users with Eco Points
  • 🔋 ReChargeBox – QR-based logging system for proper e-waste disposal
  • 💧 EcoTap – Water leak monitoring using flow sensors to prevent wastage
  • 🧠 Sortify – AI-powered scanner to help students sort waste correctly

Every action you take for the environment earns you Eco Points, which can be tracked inside the app and redeemed for rewards.

Let’s build a cleaner, greener future—one action at a time with EcoVerse. 🌍💚
